About Me

I'm Khalid Shaikh, a researcher focused on deep learning, 3D vision, and generative models. I work on reconstruction, cross-modal attention, and physics-based neural systems.

My goal is to build models that connect perception with physical understanding - making AI systems that can see, reason, and act in 3D worlds.

Research Interests

  • 3D reconstruction and NeRFs
  • Causal representation learning
  • Text-to-3D and generative modeling

Current Work

Exploring physics-guided generative systems and 4D generation and reconstruction specifically Egocentric video based reconstructions. I also study MLOps for scalable deployment of deep models.
